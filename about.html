We Are TMH Life Arena

Our Vision.

To drive towards Church excellence whilst ushering people into destiny fulfilment and preparing them for heaven.

Our Mission.

To become the global voice, along a lifelong journey of spiritual, social and economic empowerment, bringing hope and encouragement to people locally and internationally.

For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life. For God did not send his Son into the world to condemn the world, but in order that the world might be saved through him.

John 3:16-17 ESV

Our Values.

Worship

(Reaching Up): Living as a worship-driven church where God’s presence is our greatest pursuit.

Fellowship

(Reaching In): A family of believers that grows together, supports one another and cultivates strong discipleship.

Outreach

(Reaching Out): Evangelism, healing crusades and social interventions that transform lives and communities

Destiny Fulfillment

Equipping believers to discover and walk in their God-given assignments.

Excellence

Striving for spiritual, ethical, and organizational excellence
in everything we do

Holistic Ministry

Impacting lives through teaching, welfare, education, health, and social responsibility initiatives.


What We Believe.

Reaching Up (Worship):

Building a strong altar of genuine worship that ushers God’s presence into our lives and city.

Reaching In (Fellowship & Care):

Strengthening one another in love, nurturing spiritual growth, and meeting the needs of our community.

Reaching Out (Impact & Outreach):

Touching Bogoso first, then the other towns in the Western Region with the gospel through healing, deliverance, and community transformation.
